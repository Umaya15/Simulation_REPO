Welcome to Relativity Simulations, an immersive and thorough scientific platform that allows you to investigates or begin learning the principles of relativity. Relativity is a revolutionary branch and pillar of modern physics developed from the work of Henri Poincare, Hendrik Lorentz, Carl Schwarzschild, the Michelson-Morley experiment, and most notably Albert Einstein. The theory of special relativity, based on the invariance of the speed of light for all moving objects (‘frames of reference’) was the first, published in 1915.

Relativity has continued streams of advancement in scientific fields, seen in the work of Max Planck, Georges Lemaitre, Richard Feynman and Stephen Hawking. The basis for the relativity theories came from Galileo’s observations of velocity and Newton’s observations of gravity.
